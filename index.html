What is Marketing Management?

The art and strategy of understanding people's needs, wants, and how to meaningfully reach them is known as marketing management. It goes beyond simply making sales. It's all about making connections. It all comes down to understanding your audience, crafting an engaging narrative, and gradually establishing credibility. Marketing management helps you put ideas into action, whether you're managing a brand, operating a small firm, or are just getting started in the business world.

Creating value for customers while assisting in the expansion of the company is the aim of marketing management. It's about focusing on the consumer while yet making a profit.

Our Experiences

Jaycee's Experience

My experience at PHINMA COC is still ongoing, and I’ve been enjoying every part of it. During our BA Social Nights, I had so much fun—we danced and celebrated after the ceremony ended, which created unforgettable memories. I really enjoy having lunch with my classmates and friends in school; those little moments make student life even more meaningful.

Studying BSBA here at PHINMA COC has truly helped sharpen my practical and logical thinking skills, which are essential for my future goals. It’s a privilege to be part of a community where learning is deeply valued. I’ve met inspiring instructors and made friends who constantly motivate me.

COC also provides generous scholarship programs. I'm grateful to be part of the HK Scholarship, which gives me a 50% tuition discount. It has made a huge difference in easing financial concerns while still allowing me to receive quality education. I’m truly thankful to be studying here, where opportunities for growth are always within reach.
